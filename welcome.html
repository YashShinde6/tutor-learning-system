Welcome to the Tutor Management System!

We're delighted to have you on board as part of our educational community. This user-friendly platform is designed to streamline and enhance the tutoring experience for both tutors and learners.

As a tutor, you'll enjoy features such as:

    • Easy Profile Management: Update and showcase your expertise, qualifications, and availability effortlessly.
    • Smart Scheduling: Seamlessly schedule sessions with your students, keeping everyone on the same page.
    • Efficient Communication: Stay connected with your students through messaging, ensuring clear and effective communication.
    • Performance Analytics: Track your progress, monitor student feedback, and identify areas for improvement to enhance your teaching skills continually.
    • Resource Repository: Access a wealth of educational resources to enrich your teaching materials and provide an even more comprehensive learning experience.
